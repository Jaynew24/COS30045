Popular Appliances

Televisions

Modern TVs vary greatly in energy consumption. LED TVs are more efficient than Plasma or OLED models.

Average consumption: 80-400 watts

Kitchen Appliances

Refrigerators, ovens, and dishwashers are among the highest energy consumers in most households.

Average consumption: 500-2000 watts

Cooling & Heating

Air conditioners and heaters account for approximately 40% of household energy use in Australia.

Average consumption: 1000-5000 watts

Understanding Energy Consumption in Australia

Australian households spend an average of $1,500 per year on electricity, with appliances accounting for about 30% of this cost. With rising energy prices, understanding how to optimize your appliance usage can lead to significant savings.

Our website provides practical information to help you make informed decisions about your appliance usage and purchases.
