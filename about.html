About Us

Our Mission

We are a team of energy efficiency advocates based in Australia. Our goal is to provide consumers with accurate, practical information about appliance energy consumption to help reduce both electricity bills and environmental impact.

Our Goals

To educate Australian consumers about energy consumption of household appliances and promote energy-efficient choices.

Our Data

We gather information from manufacturer specifications, laboratory tests, and real-world usage studies to provide accurate consumption data.

Our Values

We believe in providing unbiased, factual information to help consumers make informed decisions without commercial influence.

Contact Us

If you have questions about appliance energy consumption or suggestions for our website, please reach out to us at info@applianceenergy.org.au
